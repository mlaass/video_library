📝 Transcript Format Instructions

The transcript file should follow this markdown format:

  • # Title - Video title (optional)
  • ## Summary - Brief summary (optional)
  • ## Transcript - Section header
  • [HH:MM:SS] Text content - Timestamped lines

Example:

# My Video Title
## Summary
This video covers important topics...

## Transcript
[00:00:05] Welcome to this presentation
[00:00:12] Today we'll discuss...
